### Class Descriptions

**Gentle Yoga** - These classes are suitable for all levels, including beginners. We focus on basic yoga postures, breathing techniques, and relaxation. Props are used to make poses accessible to everyone. The emphasis is on awareness, acceptance, and honoring your body's wisdom.

**Restorative Yoga** - A deeply relaxing practice using bolsters, blankets, and other props to support the body in restful poses. This practice helps to calm the nervous system and promote deep relaxation and healing.

**Meditation and Mindfulness** - Learn various meditation techniques including breath awareness, body scanning, and loving-kindness meditation. These practices help develop concentration, reduce stress, and cultivate inner peace.

### Workshops and Playshops

### Retreats

We occasionally offer day-long or weekend retreats that combine yoga practice with meditation, nature walks, and healthy meals. These retreats provide an opportunity to deepen your practice in a supportive, peaceful environment.

### Private Sessions

Individual yoga sessions are available for those who prefer one-on-one instruction or have specific needs or injuries. Private sessions can be tailored to your individual goals and abilities.

### Class Policies
