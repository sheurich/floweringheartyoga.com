## Welcome to Flowering Heart Yoga

My journey on the path of yoga began unexpectedly in 1971 when a friend's enthusiastic invitation brought me to a local yoga class in Bethlehem Pennsylvania where I lived at the time. Since I don't remember much about the instructor, or about the class itself, I can only imagine how much my awareness and understanding of mySelf has grown throughout this time. I do know I felt some deep connection with yoga and that relationship has continued to grow.

After the classes finished, I continued to practice what I remembered. Within a few years(1972 or 73), I took another series of classes with some of the Swamis from the Kripalu ashram in Sumneytown, Pennsylvania. This would be my first taste of rich yogic philosophy and lifestyle. I was torn between my desire to learn more and my fear of being drawn into something I wasn't ready for. I chose to continue my practice on my own.

In 1975, I moved to Maryland and began searching for yoga classes. I found a wonderful teacher, Swami Lalitananda, who had studied at the Sivananda ashram in the Bahamas. She taught a very gentle form of hatha yoga combined with pranayama, meditation and philosophy. I studied with her for about 8 years.

### Our Approach

At Flowering Heart Yoga, we believe that yoga is not about achieving perfect postures, but about developing a loving relationship with ourselves and discovering our own inner wisdom. Our classes emphasize:

- **Awareness** - Being present in each moment
- **Breath** - Using pranayama to calm the mind and energize the body  
- **Honoring the Body** - Listening to your body's wisdom
- **Self-Compassion** - Treating yourself with kindness and acceptance

### Class Offerings

We offer a variety of classes suitable for all levels:

- **Gentle Yoga** - Perfect for beginners and those seeking a slower pace
- **Restorative Yoga** - Deeply relaxing practice using props
- **Meditation** - Various techniques for developing inner peace
- **Workshops** - Special topics and intensive learning opportunities

All classes are available both in-person at our Smithsburg location and online via Zoom.
