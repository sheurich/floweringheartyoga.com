## Get In Touch

Whether you're interested in joining a class, scheduling a private session, or learning more about our programs, I'd love to hear from you. Please don't hesitate to reach out with any questions about yoga, meditation, or wellness.

I typically respond to emails within 24 hours and am happy to discuss how yoga and mindfulness practices might support your health and well-being goals.

## What to Expect

If you're new to yoga or considering your first class, here's what you can expect:

- A welcoming, non-judgmental environment
- Gentle, accessible instruction suitable for all levels
- Props provided to support your practice
- Emphasis on awareness and self-discovery rather than perfect poses
- Opportunities to ask questions and receive individual guidance

## Class Policies

- Please arrive 10-15 minutes early to settle in
- Bring a yoga mat and water bottle
- Wear comfortable, stretchy clothing
- Let me know about any injuries or health concerns
- Classes may be cancelled due to weather or low enrollment

I look forward to connecting with you and supporting you on your wellness journey!
